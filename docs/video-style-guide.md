# NewsFlow Video Style Guide

## Format

Vertical Shorts

768x1376

## Scene Count

5

## Scene Duration

4-8 seconds each

Target total:

20-45 seconds

## Transitions

Crossfade

300-500ms

## Motion

Slow zoom

Scale:

1.0 → 1.08

or

1.08 → 1.0

## Typography

Large headline

High contrast

Mobile-first readability

## Narration

Google TTS

Natural speaking rate

## Audio

Voice narration only

No background music in V1

## Objective

Professional news briefing

Not sensationalist

Not clickbait
